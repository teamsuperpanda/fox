Fox - Local-First Notes

**Short Description:** Capture your thoughts and ideas with Fox, a simple and intuitive local-first notes app.

**Full Description:**

Fox is designed for users who value privacy and simplicity. This local-first notes application allows you to create, manage, and organize your notes directly on your device, without the need for cloud synchronization or an internet connection. 

**Key Features:**

*   **Local-First Storage:** All your data is stored securely on your device, giving you complete control and privacy.
*   **Intuitive Note Management:** Easily create new notes, edit existing ones, and organize them to fit your needs.
*   **Simple and Clean Interface:** A user-friendly design ensures a smooth and efficient experience for capturing your thoughts.
*   **Offline Access:** Access and manage your notes anytime, anywhere, even without an internet connection.

Whether you're jotting down quick reminders, detailed thoughts, or creative ideas, Fox provides a straightforward and private way to stay organized. Get started today and experience the power of local-first productivity!
